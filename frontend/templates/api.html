Data schema

This project will accept data in CSV format over SSH or SFTP to a secure area within the University of Oxford. We will then process the data to a fully-anonymised format, and aggregate it. At this point it will be possible to share the anonymised data in research and chart format.

NHS organisations who wish to participate in our project should get in touch to sign off Information Governance agreements, and then can provide as much of the following information as possible, on a monthly basis. All participating organisations will then get the benefit of our monthly analyses.

During the prototype period, we are also able to help write software to convert the raw output of a LIMS to this format. Get in touch at hello@ebmdatalab.net to express an interest.
